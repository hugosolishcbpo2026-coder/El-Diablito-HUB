// Start Today

How to Get Started

1
Pick Your Tool
Choose BandLab (free, browser) to start immediately or download FL Studio / LMMS for more power.
2
Grab Free Sounds
Head to Looperman or Freesound and download free loops and samples to build your first beat.
3
Record Your Track
Use Audacity or BandLab to record your vocals or instruments over your beat.
4
Share & Collaborate
Drop your track in our community, collab with other TJ artists, get feedback and grow.
5
Release It
Use DistroKid to put your music on Spotify and Apple Music. The world needs to hear TJ's sound!
